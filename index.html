Our Commitment to Compliance

PrivacyQuote and Ainsley prioritize privacy and regulatory compliance. All SMS communications adhere to TCPA and CTIA guidelines, including:

  • Prior express written consent for all texts.
  • Double opt-in: Consumers reply "YES" to confirm participation with Ainsley.
  • Easy opt-out: Reply "STOP" to unsubscribe anytime.
  • Message frequency: Up to 15 texts per interaction; standard rates apply.
  • No data selling or sharing beyond one-to-one agent connections.
