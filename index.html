Our Commitment to Compliance

Up to 30 texts per session; Msg & data rates may apply.

PrivacyQuote and Ainsley prioritize privacy and regulatory compliance. All SMS communications adhere to TCPA, CTIA, and toll-free messaging guidelines, including:

  • Prior express written consent for all texts.
  • Double opt-in: Consumers reply "YES" to confirm participation with Ainsley.
  • Easy opt-out: Reply "STOP" to unsubscribe anytime.
  • No data selling or sharing beyond one-to-one agent connections.
  • Registered toll-free campaigns with Twilio for carrier compliance.
