SMS Compliance

PrivacyQuote, Inc. is committed to ensuring all SMS/MMS communications comply with federal and state regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), CTIA Messaging Principles, and The Campaign Registry (TCR) requirements for toll-free messaging.

1. Consent Requirements

All SMS/MMS messages sent by Ainsley, our AI assistant, require prior express written consent from recipients. Agents must ensure leads have opted in before uploading to our platform. Ainsley uses a double opt-in process (e.g., “Reply YES to continue”) to confirm consent.

2. Opt-Out Options

Recipients can opt out at any time by replying “STOP” to any message. PrivacyQuote immediately ceases communication upon receiving an opt-out request and maintains an internal suppression list to prevent further contact.

3. Message Frequency and Content

Messages are limited to 15 texts per interaction, with frequency varying but typically 10 - 15 messages. Standard message and data rates may apply. Content is restricted to insurance-related inquiries (e.g., discounts, vehicle details) and avoids prohibited topics per CTIA guidelines.

4. Agent Responsibilities

  • Verify all leads have provided prior express written consent for SMS/MMS.
  • Ensure leads are not on federal or state Do Not Call (DNC) lists before uploading.
  • Comply with state mini-TCPAs, such as Florida’s FTSA and Texas Mini-TCPA.

5. Data Protection

We do not sell or share lead data beyond facilitating one-to-one agent connections. All data is encrypted and stored securely, with access limited to authorized personnel and service providers (e.g., Twilio) under strict agreements.

6. TCR Compliance

PrivacyQuote is registered with TCR under the “Customer Care” use case, ensuring all toll-free SMS communications meet carrier standards. We maintain records of consents and opt-outs for audits.
