SMS Compliance

PrivacyQuote is committed to adhering to all applicable SMS and telemarketing regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA) and CTIA Messaging Principles. Below is an overview of our compliance practices for SMS communications with Ainsley, our AI-powered assistant.

1. Consent

All SMS communications require prior express written consent, obtained through:

  • Double opt-in: Users reply “YES” to confirm participation with Ainsley.
  • Clear disclosure in forms (e.g., Get Started page) about message frequency and purpose.
  • Integration with lead vendors ensuring prior consent for leads.

2. Opt-Out

Users can opt out at any time by:

  • Replying “STOP” to any SMS from Ainsley, immediately ceasing communications.
  • Contacting us at support@privacyquote.com.

3. Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies but typically includes 10-15 messages per interaction. Standard message and data rates may apply, as disclosed in our opt-in process.

4. Data Protection

We do not sell or share SMS data beyond one-to-one agent connections. All data is handled per our Privacy Policy.

5. State-Specific Compliance

We comply with state laws, including:

  • Florida (FTSA): No unsolicited texts; revocable consent.
  • Texas (Mini-TCPA): Adherence to consent and registration rules.
  • Washington (CEMA): Anti-spam compliance with opt-out.
  • Oklahoma (OTSA), Maryland, Michigan: State-specific telemarketing rules.
