SMS Compliance

PrivacyQuote is committed to full compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), state mini-TCPA laws, the National Do Not Call Registry (DNC), and CTIA guidelines for toll-free texting. This SMS Compliance Policy outlines our rigorous standards to protect consumers, uphold legal obligations, and secure carrier approval for our AI-powered lead nurturing platform.

1. Consent Requirements

For Agent Partners: You must ensure third-party vendors obtain Prior Express Written Consent (PEWC) from consumers requesting quotes, constituting the first opt-in. PrivacyQuote conducts a mandatory second opt-in via SMS from Ainsley (e.g., “Reply YES to confirm SMS for quote assistance”). You are liable for vendor non-compliance with PEWC.

For Consumers: Your consent is initiated via a vendor’s quote request form (first opt-in). PrivacyQuote requires a second opt-in via SMS to verify your willingness to receive messages, ensuring compliance with TCPA and CTIA.

2. Message Frequency and Content

For Agent Partners: You consent to automated SMS/MMS messages for onboarding, support, and marketing (5-10 messages per interaction, up to 20/month, varying by need).

For Consumers: Nurturing conversations with Ainsley involve <15 messages per session, ending with your STOP request, Click-to-Call, or a final message (e.g., “We’re here if you want to restart”). No further messages are sent after a session concludes.

3. Opt-Out Process

For Agent Partners and Consumers: Reply STOP to any SMS to opt out immediately. Opt-out records are retained for 5 years per TCPA § 64.1200. Contact support@privacyquote.com or 866-580-7552 to opt out. Reply HELP for support.

4. Do Not Call Compliance

For Agent Partners: You must provide consumers prescreened for DNC compliance by vendors. PrivacyQuote may implement internal DNC scrubbing via API to reject non-compliant numbers.

For Consumers: Your inclusion is contingent on vendor DNC prescreening. We maintain an internal DNC list based on your opt-out requests.

5. Messaging Hours and Timing

For Agent Partners and Consumers: Ainsley adheres to TCPA/DNC hours (8 a.m.–9 p.m. local time) for initiating SMS. Off-hours responses are treated as continuations of prior interactions, with all messages timestamped. Off-hours consumer submissions are queued until 8 a.m. local time.

6. State-Specific Compliance

We comply with mini-TCPA laws in Arizona, Connecticut, Florida, Georgia, Maryland, Mississippi, New Jersey, New York, Oklahoma, Tennessee, Texas, Utah, Virginia, Washington, and Wisconsin, requiring PEWC, time limits (e.g., 8 a.m.–8 p.m., except Texas: 9 a.m.–9 p.m. weekdays, no texts after 12 p.m. Sundays), call frequency limits (3 attempts in 24 hours), and opt-out options.

7. Security and Data Handling

We use AES-256 encryption for data transmission and storage, with regular audits. In case of a breach, we notify affected parties within 72 hours per CCPA/GDPR. We retain minimal data (phone, opt-out status) for 5 years for TCR audits.

8. Enforcement and Verification

We reserve the right to suspend messaging, terminate vendor integrations, or pursue legal remedies for non-compliance. We verify vendor PEWC via documented proofs (e.g., forms, timestamps) and may audit agent-provided consumers randomly. Non-compliance results in termination and agent liability for costs (e.g., $500–$1,500 per TCPA violation).

9. Limitation of Liability

PrivacyQuote is not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages from messaging errors or third-party non-compliance, with liability capped at fees paid in the prior 12 months or $100. Agents indemnify PrivacyQuote against claims from non-compliance.

10. Indemnification

For Agent Partners: You indemnify PrivacyQuote, its affiliates, and agents against claims, fines, and expenses (including attorney fees) from your or your vendors’ non-compliance with TCPA, CTIA, or mini-TCPA laws.

For Consumers: You indemnify PrivacyQuote against claims from inaccurate vendor-supplied data, limited to direct damages.
