SMS Compliance

At PrivacyQuote, we are unwaveringly committed to ensuring full compliance with the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), state mini-TCPA laws (e.g., Florida FTSA, Texas Mini-TCPA), the National Do Not Call Registry (DNC), and CTIA guidelines for toll-free texting. This SMS Compliance Policy outlines our rigorous standards to protect consumers, uphold legal obligations, and secure carrier approval.

Consent Requirements

For Agent Partners: You must ensure that third-party lead vendors obtain prior express written consent (PEWC) from consumers when they request a quote online, initiating the first opt-in. PrivacyQuote conducts a mandatory second opt-in verification via an initial SMS from our AI assistant, Ainsley (e.g., "Reply YES to confirm SMS for quote assistance"), to complete the double opt-in process. This double opt-in is required for all SMS communications, and you are liable for any vendor non-compliance with the initial PEWC.

For Consumers: Your consent is consumer-initiated, obtained when you provide your information to a lead vendor’s website to request a quote, constituting the first opt-in. PrivacyQuote’s second opt-in confirmation via Ainsley ensures your willingness to receive SMS. All messaging is based on this verified double opt-in, protecting your rights under applicable laws.

Message Frequency and Content

For Agent Partners: You consent to receive automated SMS/MMS messages for onboarding, support, and marketing, typically involving 5-10 messages per interaction, with an estimated total of up to 20 messages per month, subject to variation based on support needs.

For Consumers: Each nurturing conversation with Ainsley typically involves less than 15 inbound and outbound messages combined, depending on your questions, and ends with your "STOP" request, a click-to-call to your assigned agent, or a single final message (e.g., "We’re here if you want to restart") if you stop responding. No further messages are sent after a conversation concludes, and each interaction is treated separately with no aggregate limit.

Opt-Out Process

For Agent Partners: You may opt out of receiving communications at any time by replying "STOP" to any SMS message, emailing support@privacyquote.com, or calling 866-580-7552. Opt-out requests are processed immediately, and we retain records for 5 years to comply with TCPA § 64.1200.

For Consumers: You may opt out by replying "STOP" to any SMS message from Ainsley, and we honor requests immediately, retaining minimal records (e.g., phone number and opt-out status) for 5 years to ensure compliance with TCPA and DNC regulations.

Do Not Call Compliance

For Agent Partners: You are contractually required to provide leads that are prescreened for DNC compliance by vendors, ensuring all leads are free from DNC restrictions prior to integration. PrivacyQuote does not currently scrub against the DNC but may implement an optional internal prescreen via API integration to reject DNC-listed leads, enhancing compliance at our discretion.

For Consumers: Your inclusion in our system is contingent on vendor and agent compliance with DNC prescreening. We verify this compliance and maintain an internal DNC list based on your opt-out requests, processed immediately.

Messaging Hours and Timing

For Agent Partners and Consumers: Our AI, Ainsley, adheres strictly to TCPA/DNC-compliant hours (8 AM–9 PM local time) for initiating SMS communications. However, if you (agent or consumer) respond outside these hours, Ainsley may continue the conversation as a continuation of your prior initiation, with all interactions timestamped and logged for audit. For Agent Partners: Be aware that leads purchased off-hours (e.g., overnight) will be queued and not messaged until the next compliant period (8 AM local time) to ensure legal adherence, reflecting our business practice to optimize cost efficiency while maintaining compliance.

Security and Data Handling

We employ AES-256 encryption for data transmission and secure storage, conducting regular audits to prevent unauthorized access. In case of a data breach, we will notify affected parties within 72 hours as required by applicable laws (e.g., CCPA, GDPR). We retain only minimal data (e.g., phone number and opt-out status) for 5 years to meet TCPA/DNC obligations, deleting all other data upon opt-out or conclusion of a conversation.

Enforcement and Verification

PrivacyQuote reserves the unilateral right to suspend messaging, terminate vendor integrations, or pursue legal remedies if compliance is compromised. We verify vendor compliance through documented consent proofs (e.g., forms, timestamps) and may conduct random audits of agent-provided leads. Non-compliance by agents or vendors may result in immediate termination and legal action, with all costs borne by the responsible party.
