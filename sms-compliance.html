SMS Compliance

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

At PrivacyQuote, we are committed to ensuring all SMS communications comply with applicable laws and regulations, including the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), CTIA guidelines, and state-specific telemarketing laws. This SMS Compliance page outlines our practices for sending SMS/MMS messages through our AI assistant, Ainsley.

Consent Requirements

We obtain prior express written consent before sending any SMS/MMS messages. Our consent process includes:

  • Clear Disclosure: Users are informed that by providing their phone number and opting in, they agree to receive automated SMS/MMS messages from Ainsley about insurance lead nurturing or partnership inquiries.
  • Double Opt-In: After initial consent (e.g., via our Get Started form), Ainsley sends a confirmation message requiring a reply (e.g., "YES") to verify participation.
  • Opt-Out Option: Every message includes instructions to opt out by replying "STOP" and to seek help by replying "HELP."
  • Record Keeping: We maintain records of consent, including timestamps and IP addresses, to ensure compliance with TCPA and CTIA requirements.

Message Frequency and Content

Our SMS communications are designed to be clear, relevant, and compliant:

  • Frequency: Message frequency varies but typically includes up to 15 messages per interaction for lead nurturing or agent onboarding.
  • Content: Messages focus on lead engagement (e.g., confirming vehicle details, offering discounts) or agent support (e.g., discussing lead integration). No unsolicited marketing messages are sent.
  • Message and Data Rates: Standard message and data rates may apply, as disclosed in our opt-in forms.

Opt-Out Process

Users can opt out of SMS communications at any time by:

  • Replying "STOP" to any message from Ainsley, which immediately unsubscribes the user.
  • Contacting us at support@privacyquote.com or 866-580-7552 to request removal.

We honor opt-out requests promptly and maintain a do-not-contact list to prevent further messages.

State-Specific Compliance

We adhere to state-specific telemarketing laws, including but not limited to:

  • Florida (FTSA): Consent ensures no unsolicited texts, with opt-out available to avoid penalties.
  • Texas (Mini-TCPA): Compliance with registration and consent rules to protect against $500-$5,000 per violation penalties.
  • Washington (CEMA): Anti-spam compliance with opt-out options to avoid up to $2,000 per violation penalties.
  • Oklahoma (OTSA): No unsolicited texts, with opt-out to avoid $500 penalties.
  • Maryland: Compliance with the Stop Unsolicited Faxes and Calls Act.
  • Michigan: Adherence to the Home Solicitation Sales Act for opt-in and opt-out processes.

Data Protection

We protect your phone number and related data with industry-standard security measures, including encryption. We do not sell or share your data beyond facilitating one-to-one connections between leads and their providing agents. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
