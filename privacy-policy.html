Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 20, 2025

At PrivacyQuote, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you fill out forms (e.g., Contact Us or Get Started), we collect your name, email address, phone number, and state of operation.
  • Lead Information: For insurance agents, we collect data about leads you provide, such as names, contact details, and vehicle information, to facilitate SMS nurturing by our AI, Ainsley.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent, using cookies and similar technologies.
  • SMS Consent Data: When you opt in to receive SMS messages from Ainsley, we collect your consent records, including timestamps and IP addresses, to comply with TCPA and CTIA regulations.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and improve our services, including lead nurturing and personalized SMS communications.
  • Respond to your inquiries via our Contact Us form or support channels.
  • Comply with legal requirements, such as TCPA, CTIA, GDPR, and CCPA.
  • Analyze website usage to enhance functionality and user experience.
  • Facilitate Click-to-Call connections between leads and agents.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to enhance functionality and analyze trends. You can manage cookie preferences via our consent popup. Essential cookies are always active to ensure site functionality, while analytics cookies are optional and require your consent.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent (e.g., sharing lead data with the providing agent).
  • To comply with legal obligations or protect our rights.
  • With service providers (e.g., Twilio for SMS) under strict confidentiality agreements.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt out of SMS communications by replying "STOP" or contacting us.
  • Request information about data collection and usage.
  • Disable non-essential cookies via our consent popup.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@privacyquote.com.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including encryption and secure servers, to protect your data. However, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Your continued use of our services constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
