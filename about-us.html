About Us

Our Mission

At PrivacyQuote, our mission is to empower licensed insurance agents with cutting-edge technology to nurture their leads effectively and compliantly. Founded with a commitment to privacy and innovation, we leverage artificial intelligence to connect agents with consumers seeking insurance solutions, ensuring a seamless and trustworthy experience.

Our Story

Established in 2023, PrivacyQuote emerged from a vision to revolutionize lead engagement in the insurance industry. Recognizing the challenges agents face with traditional methods, we developed Ainsley, our conversational AI, to provide personalized SMS nurturing while adhering to strict regulatory standards like TCPA and CTIA. Today, we serve agents nationwide, helping them build stronger client relationships.

Our Team

Our team comprises experienced professionals in insurance, technology, and compliance. Led by industry veterans, we bring decades of expertise to ensure PrivacyQuote delivers reliable, secure, and innovative solutions. Our developers and customer support staff work tirelessly to support agents and maintain the highest standards of service.
