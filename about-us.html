About Us

Privacy Quote Inc. was founded by two seasoned insurance agents with over 50 years of combined experience. Managing approximately $60 million in premium, we’ve grown our agencies year after year by pioneering innovative approaches to lead acquisition, nurturing, and follow-up. We recognized the chaotic nature of the lead marketplace, where consumers seeking quotes are often overwhelmed by relentless phone calls—a problem that has fueled the necessity of TCPA and mini-TCPA laws.

In 2022, driven by a desire for a better solution, we began conceptualizing Ainsley, our AI-powered SMS platform. Leveraging the explosion of artificial intelligence, we’ve brought to life a vision of connecting consumers and agents through a private, friendly, and respectful method. At PrivacyQuote, we’re committed to transforming the insurance industry with technology that prioritizes consumer experience and compliance.
