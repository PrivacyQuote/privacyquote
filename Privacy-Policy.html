Privacy Policy

PrivacyQuote, Inc. (“we,” “our,” or “us”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you visit our website (privacyquote.com) or use our AI-powered SMS lead nurturing platform. We comply with federal and state privacy laws, including the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), Telephone Consumer Protection Act (TCPA), and CTIA Messaging Principles.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, address, and state of operation when you register, submit inquiries, or upload leads.
  • Lead Data: Agents upload SMS-opted-in leads (e.g., consumer names, phone numbers) for nurturing. We do not generate or sell leads.
  • Usage Data: IP address, browser type, device information, and platform interactions collected via cookies and analytics tools.
  • Sensitive Information: We do not collect sensitive data (e.g., health, financial details) unless required for insurance consulting, with your explicit consent.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Facilitating Ainsley’s SMS/MMS lead nurturing, including double opt-in consent and Click-to-Call connections.
  • Providing customer support, platform access, and analytics to improve services.
  • Comply with legal obligations, such as TCPA consent records and TCR audits.
  • Marketing and communications, only with your consent.

3. Data Sharing

We share data with:

  • Service providers (e.g., SMS platforms like Twilio) under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal authorities if required by law (e.g., subpoenas).
  • We do not sell, rent, or share lead data beyond facilitating agent-lead connections.

4. SMS/MMS Communications

We comply with TCPA and CTIA guidelines for SMS/MMS:

  • Prior express written consent is required for all automated messages.
  • Double opt-in processes confirm consent (e.g., “Reply YES to continue”).
  • Opt-out options (“Reply STOP to unsubscribe”) are honored immediately.
  • Message frequency varies but typically 10 - 15 messages per interaction; standard rates apply.
  • Agents must verify leads are not on federal or state DNC lists before uploading.

See our SMS Compliance Page for full details.

5. Data Security

We use encryption, access controls, and secure servers to protect your data. However, no system is completely secure, and you use our services at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

Under CCPA and similar laws, you may:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of data sales (we do not sell data).
  • Limit use of sensitive information.

Submit requests to support@privacyquote.com. We respond within 45 days.

7. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect their data.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in laws or practices. Updated versions will be posted here with a revised effective date.

9. Contact Us

For privacy questions, contact us at support@privacyquote.com or 866-580-7552.
